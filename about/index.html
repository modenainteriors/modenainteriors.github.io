About Us

Modena Interiors provides superior fit out and refurbishment solutions for a wide variety of clients and sectors. We provide a dedicated and professional approach and a meticulous eye for detail, seeking always to build strong and lasting relationships with our clients.

With over forty years of combined experience, our extensive portfolio ranges from the classical through to the contemporary, and covers a range of applications. We strive to ensure that throughout a project we deliver to you the highest level of service and quality.

Modena Interiors work closely with designers and architects to create visually stunning and superior quality interior fit outs throughout the UK.

Trusted by our clients for our workmanship and reliability, we make your interior fit our or refurbishment as stress free as possible so that you can focus on the important thing, keeping the business running.

At Modena Interiors, our core function is as interior fit out contractors but in collaboration with our sister company Elements we also have the skills required to create high quality bespoke joinery at a quick turnaround. We also work closely with our mechanical and engineering sub-contractors to ensure you receive the highest standard.

Modena Interiors have a very “hands on” management style to ensure your job finishes on time and in budget.

Take a look out our gallery of interior fit out work undertaken throughout the UK including restaurants and bars, hotels, shops and other retail premises, offices and private residences.
