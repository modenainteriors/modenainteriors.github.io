Hygienic Installations

Modena Interiors offer a professional and cost effective response to your hygienic wall cladding, pvc plank ceiling or Safety flooring requirement. Every project is studied and considered thoroughly to ensure that the advice, materials and workmanship which we put forward are chosen with extreme care to suit each specific installation.

Our experienced, CSCS qualified workforce installs a wide range of leading hygienic wall cladding, including Thermaclad, Altro, Whiterock and Elite pvc plus LamproSystem and Glasbord grp.
