Carpet and carpet tiles

One of the most popular requested floor coverings, carpet is chosen by many of our commercial flooring clients throughout the UK for its hardwearing, versatile & cost effective nature.

Why choose carpet flooring?

  • Carpets can make a measurable contribution to retaining the warmth in commercial areas and therefore saving on energy bills. Carpets have low heat conduction and are natural thermal insulators, creating a heat barrier.
  • Carpets have 'noise absorbing' properties, ideal for use in areas where noise pollution needs to be minimal i.e offices.
  • Safety - The soft resilient fibres of a carpet provide a cushioning effect and excellent orthopaedic properties. The non-slip and soft resilience of carpet, especially in the event of a fall, lowers the risk of injury.
  • Cleaning and maintenance of carpet is quick, easy and low cost.

Commercial Stone Flooring

The natural, tactile and aesthetic qualities of natural stone create a beautiful backdrop to any commercial flooring project. Marble, Slate, Sandstone, Limestone and Porcelain are amongst the most popular, and the choice of colours and designs is more diverse than ever.

Why choose stone for your commercial flooring needs?

  • Stone is excellent at creating a focal point.
  • Adds a natural feel to any commercial spaces such as offices and retail space.
  • Stone is extremely durable - ideal for areas of heavy footfall in commercial environments.
  • Natural stone flooring offers a clean, hard-wearing and healthy flooring option, free from dust and dirt which can affect softer floor coverings.
  • Stone is a natural material that improves with age. It weathers with wear and tear which helps contribute to its character.

Commercial Safety Vinyl Flooring

A popular choice for healthcare and sports facilities or wet areas, Vinyl offers a durable and versatile option that maintains that 'natural look'. We offer a wide range of vinyl types, styles and colours enabling us to offer you design flexibility.

Why choose safety vinyl flooring?

  • Design flexibility. Designs and shapes can be cut, meaning corporate logos and similar can be incorporated.
  • Vinyl has a natural 'give' in it making it comfortable under foot.
  • Safety. High levels of slip resistance make vinyl flooring especially useful in areas where hazards such as water or liquids may be present.
  • Vinyl is impervious to water and is stain resistant whilst being extremely easy to care for and maintain.
