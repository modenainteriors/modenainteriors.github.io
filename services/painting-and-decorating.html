Painting Services

All our painters are fully qualified in surface preparation, prior-to-paint repairs, and the correct techniques to use in different environments, from schools, to hospitals, to homes and offices. We can handle every type of painting service you might need – whether internal or external, planned or cyclical maintenance, specialist coatings or historical property redecoration. With us as your commercial painting contractor, you’ll get the right paint for the right place, applied in the right way.

We have many years’ experience using various materials of the highest quality; Dulux, Crown, Johnstones, Leyland, Sikkens, Glidden, Farrow & Ball, Laura Ashley, Fired Earth, Sandtex, Little Green.

Printed using the latest wallpaper design and print technology. Our wall papers combined with fade free inks ensure the highest resolution prints and dynamic colours guaranteed to last. Spray Painting Services

Modena Interiors provide a professional spray painting service throughout the UK. We have the top of the range HVLP and airless sprayers for all woodwork, walls, steelworks and furniture. We are able to provide spray painting for our clients if they specifically require a spray finish, and we will also advise the use of spray equipment if we feel it is appropriate for the job. We use the 3M protection system to mask and sheet up areas where required.

Our spraying services extend to both on-site and off-site depending on the job in hand. Our spraying portfolio includes; Furniture spraying, Steel spraying, Cladding spraying, MDF spraying, Interior woodwork and wall spraying, Ornate Ceiling and Suspended Ceiling spraying.

Our clients include Interiors designers, fit-out companies, private individuals, hotels and general businesses and our spraying projects vary from large commercial contracts, to single items of furniture. If you have any queries about your project, please give us a call and we'll be happy to discuss and answer any questions you may have regarding your Spray Painting Needs.
